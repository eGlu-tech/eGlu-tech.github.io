As I said in the last post, Ai is a glorified search. 
In that way, they help research; it's like instead of copying/pasting from stackoverflow, AI does it. 
But as the saying goes, "if you copy/ paste, you won't learn anything, you will feel like you are, but you aren't."

Research wise, instead of going through 10 blogs, in search of solutions, AI gives the crisp single answer.
But the problem is, apart from these solutions not 100% right, I miss those 10 opinions.
Those might not be the best, sometimes not even related, but I will learn something new, future problems, new understanding, their weaknesses, strengths, and the people who came up with them how they think.
It improves the reasoning/thinking capabilities.

I wanted to implement a simple feature-flags feature for the backend.
And as you can guess, it was a bit of a struggle. 
For the ones interested in technical parts, I have embedded the chat below.

In short, I have to switch Claude to its Opus Model with effort to high. And even with that I have to prompt it multiple times to get the right answer.
Fundamentally, I wanted to chat with it about my decisions do they make sense, better approach. Is my thought process making sense?
Like a pair programmer.
And once we settle on what to do, have it do it for me.
I wanted to use it for code generation, but each line prompt doesn't make sense. 
So let’s align what we're going to do and then let it do it.
It's not a major refactoring, but its not a trivial task also, even though it looks like one.
It requires thinking. Not that much, but understanding is required imo.
When I do things, I want to do them properly with the understanding I have and gained at that point.
It's never 100%, but I try to reach for as far as possible. 
And also more importantly, "Bare Minimum to get the job done"

In conclusion,
AI can generate code, but it cannot design. It can design those stupid "system designs" on whiteboards, those are just copy pasting at this point.
But when it comes a designing a feature which requires a deep understanding or even mid-level, it can't do.
And tbh it somewhat hinders also. Google AI mode in that content is much better, it helps in research more imo.

But personally, 

So is worth? Tbh, in cases like these it is not that much help.
But personally speaking, I like chatting with it, as a lonely person, I feel good talking to someone.
Anyway, mostly I use Ai as a pair programmer. As a lonely person, i feel good talking to it.
But how smart is it? Not that much.
Actually, how dumb it is? Well, enough to make you too...
